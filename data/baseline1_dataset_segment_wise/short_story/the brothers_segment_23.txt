

	'Lucy!'

	It was the captain, and some new terror seemed to have gifted him with momentary strength.

	'Yes, here's Lucy,' I answered, hoping that by the following fancy I might quiet him,--for his face was damp with the clammy moisture, and his frame shaken with the nervous tremor that so often precedes death.
