On Mirror Lake outspread for miles and miles,  The lotus lilies in full blossom teem.  In fifth moon Xi Shi gathers them with smiles,  Watchers o'erwhelm the bank of Yuoye Stream.  Her boat turns back without waiting moonrise  To yoyal house amid amorous sighs.\n\n\t\n\n\t\n\n\t\n\n\tReturn to the Li Bai library , or . . . Read the next poem; Ballads of Four Seasons: Winter
