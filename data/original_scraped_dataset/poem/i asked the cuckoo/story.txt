I asked the cuckoo: How many years will I live? … The tips of the pine-trees quivered, A yellow ray shone on the grass. Yet no sound in the cool grove… Now I am going home, And a refreshing breeze Kisses my burning brow.\n\n\t\n\n\t\n\n\t\n\n\tReturn to the Anna Akhmatova library , or . . .
