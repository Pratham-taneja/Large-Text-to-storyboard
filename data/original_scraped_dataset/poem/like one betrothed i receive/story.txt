Like one betrothed I receive A letter at each day's end, And late at night conceive An answer for my friend.  'On my journey to the dark, I'm staying with white death. Do no harm, my gentle one, To anyone on earth.'  Brighter, a star is shining Between that pair of trees, Calmly promising That what I dream will be.\n\n\t\n\n\t\n\n\t\n\n\tReturn to the Anna Akhmatova library , or . . .
