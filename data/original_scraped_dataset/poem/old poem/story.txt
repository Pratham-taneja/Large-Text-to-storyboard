Did Chuang Chou dream he was the butterfly? Or the butterfly dream he was Chuang Chou? In the single body's transformations See the vortex of the Myriad Creatures. No mystery then that the Magic Seas Shrank again to crystal streams, Or down by Ch'ang-an's Green Gate The gardener was Marquis of Tung-Ling.
