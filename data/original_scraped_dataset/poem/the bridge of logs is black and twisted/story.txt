The bridge of logs is black and twisted, The burdocks stand shoulder high, And a thick forest of nettles sings Of how the bright sickle will never reap here. At evening over the lake there's a sighing, And rough moss creeps along the walls.\n\n\t\n\n\t\n\n\t\n\n\tReturn to the Anna Akhmatova library , or . . .
