A Gnat flew over the meadow with much buzzing for so small a creature and settled on the tip of one of the horns of a Bull. After he had rested a short time, he made ready to fly away. But before he left he begged the Bull's pardon for having used his horn for a resting place.\n\n\t"You must be very glad to have me go now," he said.\n\n\t"It's all the same to me," replied the Bull.
