A Mule had had a long rest and much good feeding. He was feeling very vigorous indeed, and pranced around loftily, holding his head high.\n\n\t"My father certainly was a full-blooded racer," he said. "I can feel that distinctly."\n\n\tNext day he was put into harness again and that evening he was very downhearted indeed.\n\n\t"I was mistaken," he said.
